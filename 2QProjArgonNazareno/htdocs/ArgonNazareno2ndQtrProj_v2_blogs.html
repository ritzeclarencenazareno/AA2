It is undeniable that there are benefits in AI. I, however, ultimately think that the traditional way of teaching is better. Let us look at the benefits of AI in education, using AI each student will experience a unique way of teaching appropriate to their preferences and needs. Another benefit of AI in education is the speed of responses to questions from students. AI in education can also grant a more organized teaching style and schedule for the students.

(source="https://elearningindustry.com/5-main-roles-artificial-intelligence-in-education")

However, there are still disadvantages that, in my opinion, makes tradtional teaching better. First of all, human interaction is very important especially for young students. There's a higher chance of students finding it hard to communicate and open up to others in the future with less human interactions. AI in Education will also unemploy several teachers, thus, resulting in financial problems for many citizens. After weighing both the advantages and disadvantages of AI in Education, I can say that I stand by my word, traditional human-human teaching is a better option for education.
